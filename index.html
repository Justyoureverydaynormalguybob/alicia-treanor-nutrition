Welcome to Alicia Treanor Nutrition

I specialize in sports nutrition for teams and organizations, educational nutrition programs for schools, and innovative food product development. With a passion for translating nutritional science into practical applications, I help sports teams optimize performance, educate students about healthy eating, and develop market-ready healthy food products.

From working with athletic teams to delivering engaging school workshops and collaborating with food companies, I bring evidence-based nutrition expertise to groups and organizations ready to make a positive impact.
